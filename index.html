About Us

We are the Samosewala's

Welcome to Samosewala, your go-to food delivery service for delicious and authentic Indian snacks, meals and more. Our mission is to provide our customers with the convenience of ordering food online and having it delivered right to their doorstep, without sacrificing quality or taste. Our team is passionate about food and committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. We use only the freshest and highest quality ingredients in all of our dishes, and our chefs prepare each order with care and attention to detail. Whether you're craving samosas, chaat, or a hearty curry, we have a wide variety of options to choose from. We also offer vegetarian and vegan options to accommodate all dietary needs and preferences. Ordering from Samosewala is easy and convenient. Simply browse our menu online, place your order, and we'll take care of the rest. We offer fast and reliable delivery service, so you can enjoy your favorite Indian food without leaving your home or office.
